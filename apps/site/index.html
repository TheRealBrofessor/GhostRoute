GhostRoute

Record your regular drives. Save your most efficient trip as a personal reference route. Then quietly see how today's drive compares — no accounts, no cloud trip history.

iPhone Android MVP / demo status

A passive ghost reference, not a race

+0:42 behind reference the only thing GhostRoute shows while you drive

During a drive, GhostRoute shows a simple progress indicator for your saved reference trip and a real-time +/- time comparison based on route progress. That's it. No alerts, no rewards, no prompts to speed up, no competitive language — the comparison measures route efficiency (when you left, which way you went, where time is typically lost), never how fast you're moving right now. The detailed breakdown — segment-by-segment deltas, consistency score, route notes — waits until the drive has ended.

Private by design

  • No accounts, no third-party analytics, no ad SDKs.
  • Trips and location samples are stored only on your device, and only when you explicitly save them.
  • Location is sampled only while a trip is actively recording.
  • Delete any trip — or everything — in one tap from the Privacy Dashboard.
  • Optional Emergency Share (live ETA link) is opt-in per trip and hard-expires within 12 hours, server-side.

Safety posture

GhostRoute is deliberately not a racing or gamified driving app. There is no scoreboard, no "beat your time" prompt, and nothing that demands attention while driving. Post-drive insights describe where time differed; they never encourage unsafe driving.

Status

GhostRoute is an MVP. It ships with a keyless demo mode (simulated drives on sample routes) so everything works without API keys; live geocoding/routing providers (Mapbox, Google Routes, OSRM) plug in behind a provider interface and are on the roadmap.
